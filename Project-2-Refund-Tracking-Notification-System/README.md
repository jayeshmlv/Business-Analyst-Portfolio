# Refund Tracking & Notification System | Business Analyst Case Study

## Overview

This project focuses on improving refund transparency for customers of a food delivery platform by enabling refund tracking and automated status notifications.

## Business Problem

Customers were unable to track refund progress, leading to poor customer experience and increased support inquiries.

## Proposed Solution

A Refund Tracking & Notification System allowing customers to:

- Track refund status
- View refund timeline and expected refund date
- Receive refund status notifications
- Access refund history
